Business Analytics

Merchant Performance

merchant_id + transaction_date
  • Revenue: gross, net, fees
  • Volume: txn count, success rate
  • Channels: POS, online, mobile
  • Auth: 3DS authentication %
  • Payouts: settlement reconciliation
  • performance_rating
  • revenue_tier

Customer Analytics

customer_id (lifetime)
  • LTV: total transactions, spend
  • Behavior: merchants, methods
  • Recency: last 30d, 90d activity
  • Segments: whale/high/medium/low
  • Lifecycle: active/at_risk/churned
  • health_score (0–100)
  • 30% success + 20% freq + 30% recency + 20% vol
